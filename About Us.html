About Wave Cafe

The JUDY & RYAN COFFEE Shop serves as a business hub
where people from a variety of industries can gather for coffee, hold a meeting, eat lunch, or work a full day. Our skilled baristas, knowledgeable support team , and convenient location in Consolacion, Cebu City provide a lively environment for your daily business needs. Try it once, and forget the rest!

How we began

This is how began to make the wave cafe We created this wave cafe because one of my goals is to sip coffee while watching the waves of the ocean. So, with the help of Judy, I came up with the concept of creating a coffee internet shop where you can watch the waves from the comfort of your own house.
